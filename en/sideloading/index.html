Intro

Dear Players! Recently I've been forced to make changes to Groovy Labs, due to legal complaints from Meta (sic!). I'm hoping that in the coming weeks I can work out formula(s) for gameplays that will be satisfying to you and which the Meta will be OK with.

If you'd like to play the previous version of the game, that has gameplay similar to Beat Saber's, you can follow the guide below. It's a bit of a hassle, but it's the only way to play this version, as there is no chance it can be available in the official Meta Quest Store.

Prerequisites

1. Enable Developer Mode on your Quest

Follow this guide: Enable Developer Mode on Quest

Or this one: How to Turn On Developer Mode on Meta Quest 2/3/Pro

2. Install SideQuest on your PC/Mac

Go to SideQuest How-To and follow the Advanced Installer guide.

Grab the Groovy Labs APK

You can download the APKs (installable files) from the following link:

  • Groovy Labs Builds

You're specifically interested in the v1.3 release:

  • Groovy Labs v1.3 APK

Sideload (install) the APK

  1. Plug in your Oculus Quest to your computer. Connect your headset to your PC/Mac via your USB-C cable.
  2. Open SideQuest.
  3. Click Install APK from folder.
  4. Select the downloaded APK file.
  5. Wait for it to install. You can check the progress by clicking the pink '1'.
