Data Deletion for Groovy Labs

This page explains how players of Groovy Labs can request deletion of their personal data. Groovy Labs is developed by Body Mind Games.

How to request data deletion

If you wish to have your data related to Groovy Labs deleted, please send an email to: marek.stoj@bodymindgames.com.

Include the following information in your message:

  • The email address or account identifier you used in Groovy Labs,
  • A clear request to delete your data.

Once we receive your request, we will verify your identity and confirm when your data has been deleted or inform you if additional information is needed.

What data will be deleted

After verification, the following types of data will be deleted (if applicable):

  • your Groovy Labs player profile and account data,
  • game progress and associated statistics,
  • support messages and feedback linked to your account (where possible).

What data may be retained

Some data may be retained for a limited time to comply with legal, security, or operational requirements, such as:

  • records necessary for fraud prevention or legal compliance,
  • backup copies automatically overwritten after a defined retention period,
  • transactional or payment data required by the platform or tax authorities.

Once the retention period expires, any remaining data is permanently deleted or anonymized.

Developer contact

Groovy Labs is developed by Body Mind Games. For any questions or concerns about your data, please contact: marek.stoj@bodymindgames.com.
