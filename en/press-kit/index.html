Groovy Labs - The VR/MR World of Rhythm & Music

Overview

Groovy Labs is a Rhythm Games Sandbox for Virtual and Mixed Reality (VR/MR).

Though inspired by Beat Saber, we have an ambition for Groovy Labs to become something more than just a single game. We do so by providing a platform for music and rhythm-based mini-games, as well as for immersive experiences. It's all powered by content created by the developers and the community.

Groovy Labs offers a sheer number and variety of songs the players can choose from. There's tens of thousands of beatmaps (levels) created by Beat Saber community that we tap into. New songs are added on a daily basis and you can also request to create maps for your favorite songs!

Groovy Labs is highly customizable to cater to various player preferences. This includes the ability to choose the type of gameplay, an environment in which the game takes place, custom models of weapons and destructible objects and more.

The customizations described above are part of what makes Groovy Labs an extensible platform. The content that the community creates may even introduce novel gameplay mechanics that we haven't yet thought of. This approach is similar to other, highly popular platforms, like Fortnite, Minecraft or Roblox.

More information on the project website: www.groovylabsgame.com

Legal Battle with Meta

I am a solo indie developer who spent years building an original VR rhythm sandbox. Meta accused my project (formely named Beat Labs) of infringing Beat Saber and effectively forced it offline through legal pressure. While I disputed key claims, I chose to comply in good faith. I removed distribution, renamed the project to Groovy Labs, stripped all references to Beat Saber, and replaced core mechanics, assets, note systems, and even the beatmap format. The process highlighted the imbalance between an independent creator and a multibillion-dollar platform holder. In the end, after extensive changes and long dialogue, we reached an understanding. The project now moves forward independently, with a clear focus on innovation and creativity.

Audience

Groovy Labs, as a platform, aims to cater to the needs of a diverse playerbase:

  • Young adults, regular gamers, usually looking for fast-paced, reflex-base, competitive gameplay.
  • Adults, casual gamers, usually looking for more laid back, fun gameplay, In the VR/MR context commonly used as a way to get and stay fit.
  • Seniors - growing playerbase of casual gamers looking for novel, fun and relaxing gameplay that can also keep them in good shape.
